Meet Bisbee!

Hello Everyone...

I'm Bisbee, a 7 year old Exotic Shorthair cat. I was passed around 3 homes before getting rescued by my current owners in 2013. I live in SE Portland with my family of 9. My favorite family members are my Mom and Dad, but I also enjoy the birds as well: Ringo, Rosie and Siggy. I tolerate the dogs: Suede, Nimbus and Desmond, but I absolutely detest the newest member, Jerome...the other cat. My hobbies include napping on the wine rack, hiding in the fireplace, peering at you from behind a wall, knocking all objects off all surfaces, and sitting in tiny boxes.

Bisbee loves...

  • getting groomed, she loves the attention
  • when Mom puts bonito flakes on her kibble
  • feather teasers and yarn
  • long stares out the window
  • a sparkling clean litter box

Bisbee hates...

  • when Jerome chases her
  • when Jerome steals her food
  • when Jerome intrudes on her playtime
  • ...Jerome
